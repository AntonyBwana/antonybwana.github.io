Services

Eradicating youth unemployment one training at a time; with 10,000 youths trained since our inception in 2015.

Entrepreneurship Training

We offer entrepreneurship training for those who want to venture into self employment and start their own unique journey so that they can create job opportunities. You can join the training to unlock your full potential.

Business Incubation.

We offer startups incubation for new enterprises by walking with them through the business development from ideation to pitching for funds.

Business Accelerator

We offer these service to micro-entrepreneurs who have already have running businesses but are looking for growth and scale.

ICT and Innovation

We offer ICT integration support to startups so that they can have a digital presence in the market place looking at 2050 vision where everything will be digital.

Business Fundraising

We help startups access funds after proving market validation of product or service using different channels like grants,loans or equity.

Business Development support (BDS) and Mentorship

We offer business development support and mentorship through our Alumni network by following up on existing businesses and making sure they are successful.
