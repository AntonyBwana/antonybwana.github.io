About Us

Enabling and Empowering youths

We are a new breed of youth who envisions a society where we care for one another, where we value the elderly for their wisdom, where we embrace natural resources and use technology to utilize the opportunities that lie around us. We work hard to make a profitable business. Not to enrich ourselves, but to create jobs, to decrease the gap between rich and poor, and to increase food security for generations to come. We don’t discriminate on gender, tribal origin or religion, and we have zero tolerance of corruption. We are a new kind of tribe that exceeds all traditional tribes in our country.

“We are Vijana Reloaded.

Our Creative Team

This is the team making youths dream of empowerment possible.

Ronald Mukanga

Co-Founder/Executive Director

He is a social entrepreneur with a background in business economics and he is passionate about entrepreneurship and social enterprise development. Ronald believes that entrepreneurship will help poverty eradication by solving critical global problems and create employment opportunities to the youths and underprivileged communities in the society. Ronald has vast experience in business development and entrepreneurship training with more than 17 years in the industry. He is also a transformative life and business coach.

Evans Okacha

COO

Evans is passionate about youth empowerment, entrepreneurship and business development. He believes that if youths can embrace agribusiness, the world can achieve zero hunger and create jobs solving the youth unemployment problem. Having developed a successful agribusiness enterprise he is convicted that many other youths can change their lives through entrepreneurship rather than waiting to be employed with no jobs available. Evans has a background in Human centered design, Lean startup methodology and ICT with more than 8 years experience in entrepreneurship training. His purpose is to impact the youth by sharing his experience and knowledge, mentorship and meaningful connection providing them with alternative perspectives about life. He can only stop when there is no single young person without a thriving livelihood.

Maureen Amakabane

Business development

Maureen is extremely passionate about women and their socio-economic advancement. She believes women can be able to achieve socio-economic empowerment by leveraging on entrepreneurship as a catalyst. She has a background in business development, entrepreneurship and leadership. Maureen is a serial entrepreneur with startups in sanitation and business consulting. Her purpose is to encourage women to shift their mindset into entrepreneurship and create businesses with the intention of solving unemployment problems and financial freedom.

Lydia Munika

Head Trainer

Lydia is passionate about seeing youth and women grow in their business and improve their livelihoods. She has a background in business and economics. With over 15 years experience in training with an emphasis on Entrepreneurship training. Both nationally and internationally. Lydia has over 3 years in banking and finance. She cherishes her responsibilities as a trainer, mentor and coach. Her purpose is to change one life at a time by creating an entrepreneurship mindset as a springboard for social-economic empowerment.

Antony Bwana

CTO

Anthony is our technology and innovation executive responsible for developing organizational strategy for using technological resources. Ensuring digital integrations of systems and infrastructure. He is also responsible for technological evaluation and implementation. He has a background in Information Communication Technology(ICT). He is passionate about solving global challenges using innovative technologies.
